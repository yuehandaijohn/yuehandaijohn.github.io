10 youtube channels msut check out if you are or wanted to be an entrepreneurs
Startup
February 11, 2024
overview

From your first day as an entrepreneur you will encounter all sorts of challenges, the following 10 youtube channels contain all sorts of guidance you need to start your own business, from Idea to IPO.

Founder Institute

The World’a largest startup support organization, has videos and live for all kind of startups.

modern MBA

Contains a lot of brand story and knowleges.

Roger Royse Law

From start-up technology companies to publicly traded multinationals. Teaching and counseling services for complex tax structures, high-stakes business negotiations and large international financial transactions.

Platform Engineering

Focused on platform and software development type startups explaining with their own online community of programmers.

Y Combinator

Has superb business knowledge along with various programs to support entrepreneurs.

GaryVee

If you are someone who loves listening to popcasts while enjoying entrepreneurship and business, then this channel will definitely become your favorite.

Startup Grind

For those who can sit down and spend an hour watching a video then this channel is definitely a presence you should check out. They interview various entrepreneurs and self-produce various programs.

TechCrunch

TechCrunch is a leading technology media platform, and their YouTube channel covers the latest news, trends, and analyses in the tech startup ecosystem, including startup profiles and interviews.

Foundr

Foundr is a media and education company focused on entrepreneurship. Their YouTube channel features interviews with successful entrepreneurs, as well as practical tips and strategies for starting and growing a business.

Stanford eCorner

Stanford eCorner is the entrepreneurship corner at Stanford University, and their YouTube channel features lectures, interviews, and talks from industry experts, academics, and successful entrepreneurs on various aspects of starting and scaling a business.
